    A new "home" for fnl.es

    After months of looking for a loophole in my calendar to migrate my slightly outdated and mostly untended Google Blogger blog to something more modern, I have found the ideal timing: The birth and arrival of my son, Alexander LV (@AlexThe55th)!

    The content will remain similar to earlier posts, while I will try to reduce the amount of private stuff and augment the articles about (mostly bio-) data mininig issues, in particular text mining and software engineering solutions. Now and then I intend to throw in some issues about privacy, human rights, and other things that bother me. And then one or the other "lifehacker" tip whenever appropriate.

    As blogging framework I have decided to move to Pelican, because it is Python-based, generating extremely light-weight static pages from ReST, and thus allows me to easily maintain the blog from any terminal and its pythonic origins allow me to hack the framework if need be.
