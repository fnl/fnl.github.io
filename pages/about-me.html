About Me

Welcome, stranger. I am Florian Leitner, an Austrian living in his exile of choice: Madrid, Spain. I dedicate myself to Molecular Biology, particularly Bioinformatics (If I correctly understand the current "Big Data" hype, I am a "Data Scientist" for Molecular Biology - which indicates that we Bioinformaticans could well be one of the most ancient Data Scientists around…). My area of interest and PhD has been Text Mining, obviously for Molecular Biology, and I have a healthy apetite for means that make machines more like humans, meaning Artificial Intelligence.

For news and updates, feel free to follow me on Twitter (@flowing). If you wish to contact me, get hold of me via e-mail, please. I still am managing to avoid the total exposure of my private life to governmental organizations and transnational cooperations that you (maybe inconciously) subdue yourself by owning a riduculously overpriced "smart" phone (And no, I am not paranoid, there is hard evidence for the tracking and the sniffing going on - Huxley and Orwell didn't even scratch the surface of what was to come…) So please abstain from "Whats-Apping" me or forcing me to contribute content to other social menances (tellingly called "networks") that are making people at dinner tables being more attentive to these Big Brother gadgets than acutal real-life happening around them.
