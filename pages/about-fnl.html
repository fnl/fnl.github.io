  1. About fnl

    Welcome, stranger. I am Florian "fnl" Leitner (aka. flowing), an Austrian living in his exile of choice: Madrid, Spain. I dedicate myself to Molecular Biology, particularly Bioinformatics (If I correctly understand the current "Big Data" hype, I am a "Data Scientist" for Molecular Biology - which indicates that we Bioinformaticans could very well be one of the most ancient Data Scientists around…) My area of interest and PhD has been Text Mining, obviously for Molecular Biology, and I have a healthy apetite for means that make machines "think" like humans, meaning Artificial Intelligence.

    In general, I belive the declaration of the Human Rights to have been the bigget achievement of humanity so far. Also, I am a vivid defender of personal privacy, and a supporter of open source and the copyleft movement. Over time, you will find content about these topics on this blog.

    For these reasons, I avoid the total exposure of my private life to governmental organizations and transnational cooperations that you (maybe unconciously) subdue yourself by owning a (in addition, riduculously overpriced) "smart" phone. And no, I am not being paranoid: There is hard evidence for the tracking and the sniffing going on - Huxley and Orwell didn't even scratch the surface of what was to come… So there is no use in "Whats-Apping" me or trying to convince me to contribute content to people monitoring (tellingly called "social network") sites that on top of all are making us more attentive to our gadgets at social gatherings than to the acutal real-life going on around us.

    For news and updates, feel free to follow me on Twitter (@flowing). If you wish to contact me, get hold of me via e-mail, please. For my professional details, please have a look at the PDF of my CV.
